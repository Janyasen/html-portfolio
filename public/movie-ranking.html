The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Grave of the Fireflies

This is a beautifully done anime. The artwork is gorgeous. The story is very sad, and I wish world leaders and people in general would see this movie and decide that humanity really needs to do better, and treat each other in a more humane manor.

Dune

This 2021 version of Dune is the first film version of this story that portrayed it exactly as I imagined it while reading the book.

The Witch

I really enjoyed how well this film depicts how religion is the cause for so much oppression, misogyny and fear.
